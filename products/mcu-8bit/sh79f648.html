SH79F648

High-performance MCU for air conditioner and white goods control applications

8-bit MCU SH79F Series

Product Overview

The SH79F648 is a high-performance 8-bit microcontroller based on the enhanced 8051 core, specifically designed for air conditioner and white goods control applications. It features advanced motor control peripherals and multiple communication interfaces, making it ideal for sophisticated home appliance control systems.

At Litong, we provide comprehensive support for the SH79F648, including technical assistance, sample provisioning, and competitive pricing for your design-in needs. Our FAE team has extensive experience with SH79F648 applications in air conditioners, refrigerators, and other white goods.

FAE Technical Note

SH79F648 is ideal for air conditioner applications with its advanced motor control peripherals and communication interfaces. FAE recommendation for white goods with variable-speed motors. For optimal motor control performance, we recommend using the dedicated PWM modules and implementing proper dead-time control in your motor drive algorithms.
