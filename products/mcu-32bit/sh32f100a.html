SH32F100A

High-performance ARM Cortex-M0 MCU for smart home and IoT sensor applications

32-bit MCU SH32F Series

Product Overview

The SH32F100A is a high-performance 32-bit microcontroller based on the ARM Cortex-M0 core, designed for smart home and IoT sensor applications. It offers enhanced processing power, memory capacity, and peripheral integration compared to 8-bit solutions, making it ideal for connected devices requiring moderate computational resources with low power consumption.

At Litong, we provide comprehensive support for the SH32F100A, including technical assistance, sample provisioning, and competitive pricing for your design-in needs. Our FAE team has extensive experience with SH32F100A applications in smart home devices, IoT sensors, and connected appliances. The SH32F100A is particularly well-suited for battery-powered applications where the balance of performance, peripheral integration, and power efficiency is critical.

FAE Technical Note

SH32F100A is ideal for battery-powered IoT applications where the balance of performance, peripheral integration, and power efficiency is critical. For optimal power efficiency, we recommend using the power management features and implementing sleep modes appropriately. The integrated 12-bit ADC with 16 channels makes this MCU excellent for sensor applications requiring high-precision measurements.
