Sinowealth BMS Solutions

Advanced battery management chips for e-bike, power tools, and energy storage applications

About Sinowealth BMS Chips

Sinowealth's BMS chips, particularly the SH36700 series, have gained significant market share in the e-bike segment due to their high precision and reliability. These chips support up to 13 series battery cells with integrated analog front-end for voltage, current and temperature monitoring.

Our BMS solutions encompass not just the core IC but also reference designs, software libraries, and technical support for quick time-to-market. The SH36700 series is especially popular for e-bike, power tool, and energy storage applications due to their comprehensive protection features and high integration. These chips provide essential protection functions including overcharge, overdischarge, overcurrent, and short-circuit protection while maintaining precise cell voltage monitoring.
