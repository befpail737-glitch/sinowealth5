SH36750 Series

High-integration BMS for energy storage and industrial applications

BMS IC SH36750 Series

Product Overview

The SH36750 Series is a high-integration battery management system (BMS) IC designed for energy storage and industrial applications requiring enhanced reliability and extended temperature range operation. It supports up to 20 series-connected cells and features high-precision current sensing, making it ideal for industrial energy storage systems, telecommunications backup power, and other demanding applications.

At Litong, we provide comprehensive support for the SH36750 Series, including technical assistance, sample provisioning, and competitive pricing for your design-in needs. Our FAE team has extensive experience with SH36750 applications in industrial energy storage and telecommunications power systems. The enhanced reliability features and extended temperature range make this series particularly suitable for applications in harsh environments where consistent performance is critical.
