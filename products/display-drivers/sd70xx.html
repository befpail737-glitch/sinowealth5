SD70xx Series

Advanced AMOLED driver with enhanced color management for premium displays

Display Driver IC SD70xx Series

Product Overview

The SD70xx Series is an advanced AMOLED display driver IC designed for premium displays, featuring enhanced color management and support for up to QHD resolution. This series provides superior image quality, advanced color gamut support, and adaptive brightness control, making it ideal for high-end smartphones, tablets, and other premium mobile devices.

At Litong, we provide comprehensive support for the SD70xx Series, including technical assistance, sample provisioning, and competitive pricing for your design-in needs. Our FAE team has extensive experience with SD70xx applications in premium display systems. The enhanced color management and EMI reduction features make this series particularly suitable for applications where visual fidelity and regulatory compliance are critical.

SD70xx Series is ideal for premium display applications where enhanced color reproduction and HDR support are critical. The advanced color management features enable wider color gamuts for more vivid visuals. For optimal performance, pay special attention to PCB layout guidelines to maintain signal integrity for high-frequency data transmission. The EMI reduction features help meet regulatory requirements for consumer electronics.
