About Sinowealth Display Drivers

Sinowealth's display driver solutions address the growing demand for OLED and AMOLED displays in mobile, IoT, and consumer electronics. Their driver ICs provide excellent image quality, low power consumption, and support for various resolution standards.

Our technical support includes panel compatibility guidance, design-in support, and customization services for specific applications. These drivers are ideal for smart watches, mobile phones, automotive displays, and other applications requiring high quality visual output. Sinowealth's display drivers offer competitive performance and power efficiency compared to other solutions in the market.

FAE Technical Note

Sinowealth's SD60xx series offers an excellent balance of performance and power efficiency for mainstream smartphone displays. For applications requiring higher resolutions or enhanced color reproduction, consider the SD70xx series. The SD80xx series is designed for automotive and industrial applications where extended temperature range and reliability are critical. When designing in Sinowealth display drivers, pay particular attention to PCB layout guidelines to ensure signal integrity and minimize EMI.
