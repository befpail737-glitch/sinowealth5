How to Select Sinowealth Products

Selecting the right semiconductor product is critical to the success of your application. This guide provides a systematic approach to choosing Sinowealth MCUs, BMS chips, and display drivers based on your specific requirements. Consider the following factors when selecting products:

Application Requirements Analysis

Different appliance applications have varying requirements for processing power, peripherals, and integration. Consider these factors when selecting your MCU:

  • Electromagnetic stoves: Require precise ADC for temperature sensing and multiple PWM channels
  • Rice cookers: Need touch buttons, LCD control, and timer functions
  • Air conditioners: Require motor control capabilities and communication interfaces
  • Water heaters: Focus on safety features and temperature control

Key Selection Factors

Processing Power

8-bit MCUs (SH79F series): Ideal for cost-sensitive applications with moderate computational needs like home appliances. 32-bit MCUs (SH32F series): Better for applications requiring more computational power, complex algorithms, or higher peripheral integration.

Power Consumption

For battery-powered applications, consider the power modes and consumption profiles of different devices. Our 32-bit MCUs offer advanced power management features for IoT applications, while 8-bit options provide excellent efficiency for continuously powered devices.

Peripherals & Integration

Evaluate the built-in peripherals like ADCs, PWM units, communication interfaces (UART, I2C, SPI), and specialized units (touch sensing, LCD drivers) that match your application needs.

Cost Considerations

Balance performance requirements with cost targets. Our SH79F series offers excellent cost-performance ratio for home appliances, while SH32F provides more features for more complex applications.

Supply & Availability

Consider long-term availability, supply stability, and lifecycle management. As an authorized distributor, we provide consistent supply for all Sinowealth products.

Development Resources

Evaluate the availability of development tools, documentation, and technical support. Sinowealth products are supported by standard development environments like Keil for 8-bit and ARM tools for 32-bit MCUs.

FAE Recommendation

For home appliance applications: The SH79F series provides the best cost-performance ratio, especially for applications like rice cookers, electromagnetic stoves, and air conditioners. For more complex IoT applications: Consider the SH32F series when you need more computational power, advanced communication features, or larger memory. For BMS applications, SH36700 is the standard choice for e-bike applications, while SH36720 is recommended for smart battery systems with communication requirements.
