About Litong

Authorized Sinowealth Distributor Providing Technical Excellence Since 2010

Our Story

Founded with the vision of bridging advanced semiconductor technologies with market applications, Litong has grown to become a leading authorized distributor for Sinowealth products. Our journey began in the early 2010s when we recognized the potential of Sinowealth's innovative MCU solutions in the rapidly expanding home appliance market.

Today, we serve customers across multiple regions, providing not just components but comprehensive solutions that include technical support, design-in assistance, and supply chain management. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the recognition as an authorized Level 1 Distributor for Sinowealth, the leading supplier of 8-bit and 32-bit MCUs in China.

Our success is built on a foundation of deep technical expertise, reliable supply, and unwavering customer focus. We continue to invest in our technical capabilities and supply chain to better serve the evolving needs of our customers in the electronics industry.

Our Team & Expertise

Our team combines deep expertise in Sinowealth products with extensive experience in application development. Our Field Application Engineers (FAEs) hold degrees in relevant engineering disciplines and possess years of experience in MCU applications across various market segments.

Key team capabilities include:

  • Application-specific design support
  • Technical training and workshops
  • System-level integration guidance
  • Post-design-in production support
  • Supply chain optimization

Our FAE team is available to support customers from initial concept through mass production, ensuring successful implementation of Sinowealth solutions.
